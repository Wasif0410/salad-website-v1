## Brand Summary

SALAD Language School is an online language-learning institution focused on French and English training, with Spanish planned as an upcoming expansion. The school positions itself as professional, accessible, live, and human. Its strongest differentiator is a creative teaching philosophy influenced by the founder's music and production background: language is taught with attention to rhythm, flow, melody, confidence, and natural expression.

The brand should feel:

- Premium but approachable
- Warm, structured, and credible
- More adult/professional than childish classroom
- Online-first, not location-based
- Useful for both individuals and organizations

## Hero Image Direction

Best fit for the current template:

Create a full-screen, layered hero image with a refined online language-learning scene. It should use warm cream, burgundy, gold, and muted green/olive accents while leaving clean negative space for centered overlay text. The image should feel like a premium educational brand, not a stock classroom.

Prompt base:

> Premium full-screen website hero image for SALAD Language School, an online French and English language school. Refined palette of deep burgundy, warm gold, sage green, cream, and soft warm natural light. Clean spacious composition with depth, suitable for a full-screen website hero with centered text overlay and generous empty space in the upper and central area.
